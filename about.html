About Me

Hi, I'm Eyob Legese, a Front-end developer at Alphaworks. I have two years of hands-on experience building and designing websites and mobile applications using the latest technologies in web development. Prior to web development, I earned a BA degree in Accounting and Finance from CalPoly, Pomona.

Following graduation, I worked for almost four years as a staff accountant for a financial services company. Despite my passion for analyzing numbers, web development turned out to be my true calling. During the 2020 Covid lockdown, I began taking web development courses out of curiosity. It wasn't until I learned about HTML that I realized web development would be a more creative career path for me. As my skills improved, I took more advanced courses in web development. Within a year of starting my career as a Front-end developer, I was accepted to a react Native apprenticeship program at Alphaworks.

I'm applying my newly acquired skills to develop more creative and appealing mobile applications and websites. I'm passionate about turning concepts into something very useful.
